Hi, my name is JP.

I'm a designer.

You say multimedia, I say "aye!". At different points in time, I've been graphic designer, fashion designer, retail product designer, and now web designer and developer.


I create web and graphic design assets. I am most valuable in projects where I create illustrative design elements, digital and traditional illustration, social media content creation, and logo design.


I have formal training in visual arts, with a focus on advertising. I bring with me years of art practice with a solid foundation in theories of color, composition and practical design. I have fundamental understanding and practical experience of creating websites.


We live in a world now where adapting and thriving digitally is essential for professionals and businesses. But I believe it shouldn't be at the cost of who you really are, of your true story. Let's design around your story and make sure it's out there, connecting with the people who matter.
